# Kerala Friends Trip — Munnar & Alleppey

4 budget-friendly nights through tea hills and backwaters.

## Day 1 — Arrival in Munnar
- **09:00 AM** Arrival at Cochin Airport
- **12:00 PM** Drive to Munnar (4 hr scenic)
- **04:00 PM** Resort check-in
- **06:00 PM** Tea estate walk
- **08:00 PM** Welcome dinner

## Day 2 — Munnar Sightseeing
- **08:00 AM** Breakfast
- **09:30 AM** Eravikulam National Park
- **12:00 PM** Tea Museum visit
- **02:00 PM** Lunch
- **04:00 PM** Mattupetty Dam & Echo Point
- **08:00 PM** Dinner

## Day 3 — Drive to Alleppey
- **08:00 AM** Breakfast & check-out
- **10:00 AM** Drive to Alleppey (4 hr)
- **02:00 PM** Houseboat check-in
- **03:00 PM** Backwater cruise begins
- **08:00 PM** Kerala dinner on board

## Day 4 — Houseboat Cruise
- **08:00 AM** Breakfast on board
- **10:00 AM** Continue cruising through villages
- **12:00 PM** Houseboat check-out
- **02:00 PM** Drive to Cochin
- **06:00 PM** Hotel check-in Cochin

## Day 5 — Cochin & Departure
- **09:00 AM** Breakfast
- **10:00 AM** Fort Kochi walk (Chinese fishing nets, Jewish Synagogue)
- **01:00 PM** Lunch
- **03:00 PM** Transfer to airport
- **06:00 PM** Departure

- 2 nights resort in Munnar

- 1 night hotel in Cochin
- Daily breakfast (dinner on houseboat)
- All transfers in private vehicle
- All sightseeing entry tickets

- Personal expenses
