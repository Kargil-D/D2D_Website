# Bangkok and Pattaya Combo

A perfect blend of city vibes and beach fun - explore Bangkok's temples and night markets, then unwind in Pattaya.

## Day 1 - Arrival in Bangkok
- **10:00 AM** Arrive at Suvarnabhumi Airport
- **12:00 PM** Transfer to hotel and check-in
- **04:00 PM** Visit Asiatique Riverfront
- **08:00 PM** Welcome dinner

## Day 2 - Bangkok City Tour
- **08:00 AM** Breakfast
- **09:30 AM** Grand Palace and Wat Pho
- **01:00 PM** Lunch
- **04:00 PM** Chao Phraya river cruise

## Day 3 - Transfer to Pattaya
- **08:00 AM** Breakfast
- **10:00 AM** Coach transfer to Pattaya
- **02:00 PM** Hotel check-in
- **07:30 PM** Alcazar cabaret show

## Day 4 - Coral Island
- **08:00 AM** Breakfast
- **09:30 AM** Coral Island tour with water sports
- **04:00 PM** Walking Street stroll

## Day 5 - Departure
- **08:00 AM** Breakfast and check-out
- **11:00 AM** Transfer to Bangkok airport
- **02:00 PM** Departure

## Inclusions
- 2 nights Bangkok + 2 nights Pattaya
- Daily breakfast
- Coral Island tour with lunch
- Alcazar show tickets
- All transfers

## Exclusions
- International airfare
- Lunch and dinner (except mentioned)
- Personal expenses
- Travel insurance
