# Phuket Island Hopper

Five nights chasing the Andaman's most iconic islands - powder beaches, limestone karsts and turquoise water.

## Day 1 - Arrival
- **10:00 AM** Arrive at Phuket Airport
- **12:00 PM** Transfer to beach resort and check-in
- **05:00 PM** Sunset at Patong Beach
- **08:00 PM** Welcome dinner

## Day 2 - Phi Phi Islands
- **08:00 AM** Breakfast
- **09:00 AM** Speedboat to Phi Phi Islands
- **01:00 PM** Lunch on Phi Phi Don
- **04:00 PM** Maya Bay viewpoint

## Day 3 - James Bond Island
- **08:00 AM** Breakfast
- **09:30 AM** Phang Nga Bay and James Bond Island tour
- **01:00 PM** Lunch
- **04:00 PM** Canoeing in the caves

## Day 4 - Coral Island
- **08:00 AM** Breakfast
- **10:00 AM** Coral Island snorkeling
- **02:00 PM** Beach time
- **08:00 PM** Seafood dinner

## Day 5 - Phuket City
- **08:00 AM** Breakfast
- **10:00 AM** Old Phuket Town walk
- **02:00 PM** Big Buddha viewpoint
- **08:00 PM** Farewell dinner

## Day 6 - Departure
- **08:00 AM** Breakfast and check-out
- **11:00 AM** Transfer to airport
- **02:00 PM** Departure

## Inclusions
- 5 nights beach resort
- Daily breakfast
- Phi Phi Islands tour with lunch
- James Bond Island tour
- All transfers

## Exclusions
- International airfare
- Lunch and dinner (except mentioned)
- Personal expenses
- Travel insurance
