# Northern Thailand Explorer

Five culture-rich nights through Chiang Mai's temples and Chiang Rai's golden landscapes - with ethical elephant moments along the way.

## Day 1 - Arrival in Chiang Mai
- **11:00 AM** Arrive at Chiang Mai Airport
- **01:00 PM** Hotel check-in
- **04:00 PM** Old City walking tour
- **07:30 PM** Sunday night market

## Day 2 - Doi Suthep Temple
- **08:00 AM** Breakfast
- **09:30 AM** Doi Suthep temple visit
- **01:00 PM** Lunch
- **04:00 PM** Hill tribe village stop

## Day 3 - Elephant Sanctuary
- **08:00 AM** Breakfast
- **09:00 AM** Ethical elephant sanctuary visit
- **02:00 PM** Lunch with mahouts
- **08:00 PM** Khantoke dinner show

## Day 4 - Transfer to Chiang Rai
- **08:00 AM** Breakfast
- **10:00 AM** Coach to Chiang Rai
- **02:00 PM** Hotel check-in
- **04:00 PM** Night Bazaar exploration

## Day 5 - Temples of Chiang Rai
- **08:00 AM** Breakfast
- **09:30 AM** White Temple (Wat Rong Khun)
- **01:00 PM** Lunch
- **03:00 PM** Blue Temple and Black House

## Day 6 - Departure
- **08:00 AM** Breakfast and check-out
- **11:00 AM** Transfer to Chiang Rai airport
- **02:00 PM** Departure

## Inclusions
- 3 nights Chiang Mai + 2 nights Chiang Rai
- Daily breakfast
- Elephant sanctuary visit
- All sightseeing and transfers

## Exclusions
- International airfare
- Lunch and dinner (except mentioned)
- Personal expenses
- Travel insurance
